What is steroid?
Steroids are a man-made version of hormones normally produced by the adrenal glands which are 2 small glands found above the kidneys.
Steroids reduce redness and swelling (inflammation). This can help with inflammatory conditions such as asthma and eczema.

What is bad thing of steroid?
Athletes often misuse steroids to strengthen their muscles, and it is a doping drug.
Therefore, it is forbidden for athletes to take them.
There are serious side effects. A typical side effect is Cushing syndrome as a chronic side effect.
As the health club population has recently increased, cases of illegally purchasing and using anabolic steroids for rapid muscle mass increase and side effects are increasing.
